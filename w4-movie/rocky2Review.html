Review

Charles LaRose April 22, 2026


Rocky II is an okay sequel but has flaws. The film is very predictable in some regards. The story in between the fight is very intriguing as we watch Rocky fall from the high he was after the first film. Struggling to get by after blowing through all his new found fortune and unable to get a job.

We see Rocky and Adrian get married as well as having a baby boy named Robert. However Adrian falls into a coma due to the stress of Rocky fighting again. Eventually though she wakes up and tells Rocky to go out there and win. From there we get the best training montage in the series and the iconic “Gonna Fly Now” by Bill Conti is played. All the Rocky films have great soundtracks with the majority of them being music composed by Bill Conti. You will be hard pressed to find an American who doesn’t know the Rocky theme song.

Now my biggest issue with this film is the predictability of it. Of course Rocky wins his rematch with Apollo as it would be impossible for a sequel like this to have any other outcome. In a way this obvious ending makes watching the film feel like a waste. The story in between the fight is also not the best of the series in my opinion.
